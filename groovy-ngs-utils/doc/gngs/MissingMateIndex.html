        • java.util.PriorityQueue<SAMRecordPair> resolveQueue

          A list of unresolved read pairs (that is, where one pair is missing) ordered by the priority of resolving them.

          We assume that genome is being scanned in sorted order, and thus the priority is to resolve the "left most" or lowest genomic position first, as this is most likely to hold up scanning.
